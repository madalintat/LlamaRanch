Quality

Know what you're really riding.

A local model is a shrunk model, and almost nothing tells you how much sharpness it lost. The ranch measures it, on your own machine, and shows it as a plain grade. It names the sweet spot for your hardware, and when two sizes are too close to call, it says so. Measured once on the night shift, remembered forever.
